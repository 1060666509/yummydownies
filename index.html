Privacy Policy for Yummy Downies

Last updated: November 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Yummy Downies! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while playing our game.

Information We Don't Collect

Yummy Downies is a simple game that respects your privacy. We do not collect any personal information. The game operates entirely on your device, and we do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts or photos
  • Share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

The only data we store is your game scores and settings, which are saved locally on your device. This data is used solely to:

  • Display your game scores
  • Maintain your game settings
  • Show your ranking history

This data remains on your device and is automatically removed if you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. Your game data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

App Permissions

Yummy Downies only requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your game scores and settings locally
  • Audio: To play game sounds and background music

Third-Party Services

Our game does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted over the internet. This ensures your game data remains private and secure.

Conclusion

Yummy Downies is designed with privacy in mind. We believe in creating a fun gaming experience while respecting your privacy. Thank you for trusting us with your gaming experience!
